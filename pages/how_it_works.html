    How it works

    • Limedot is a loan platform where private investors from all over Europe (and outside) can invest in loans issued by our partners – Loan Originators.

    • Limedot operates and manages a platform for investing in loans, connecting the investors, borrowers, and Loan Originators. The minimum investment is as little as 10€.

    • Investing in loans refers to individual investors providing money for borrowers through Loan Originators by purchasing the loan parts (claim rights).

    How to start investing

    Anyone who is 18 years old and possesses a bank account within any EU country or has cryptocurrency stablecoins account is eligible to register.

    Limedot features at a glance

    Secondary market

    The secondary market offers the opportunity to sell the investment before its maturity. Selling on the secondary market is completely free with us and helps in the situation when you need a fast exit

    Auto-Invest

    Auto-Invest feature allows investing automatically without wasting your precious time. All you need to do is set the criteria matching your investment goals and appetite, and the Auto-Invest will invest on your behalf

    Group guarantee

    For Limedot loans, we have a Group Guarantee, meaning that in case the Loan Originator cannot fulfill its BuyBack Obligation towards investors, Limedot steps in to fulfill the BuyBack Obligation.

    Buyback obligation

    Investing in unsecured personal loans can be risky, as the borrowers may default on the loan. Therefore almost all Loan Originators give a guarantee in the form of BuyBack Obligation.
